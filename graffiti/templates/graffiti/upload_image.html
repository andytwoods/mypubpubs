Welcome to StencilAR

Once you have added a URL, please follow instruction in StencilAR to download that URL to your headset. The software will then be able to download your image. One way of creating a URL for your image is to upload it to dropbox and ask dropbox to generate a URL (see here on how to do this with onedrive). By saving over the same image in dropbox/onedrive, you can get StencilAR to download each change, without needing to return here!

By using this web application, you acknowledge that:
  • URLs saved here are publicly available
  • Headset IDs are stored alongside URLs to help keep this site safe. All data will also be periodically deleted from our database Click to delete all data now
